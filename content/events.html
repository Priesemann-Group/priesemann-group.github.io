Research ethics of social media research Nov 14, 2025

Dept. of Medical Ethics and History of Medicine, Seminar room & library, Humboldtallee 36

We invite interested researchers to a interdisciplinary workshop where we want to discuss current issues and future tasks to address research ethical aspects of social media research in mixed research settings from sociology, ethics, data science, neuroscience, political science, legal studies etc.

Venue

Dept. of Medical Ethics and History of Medicine, Seminar room & library, Humboldtallee 36

Date

Friday, Nov 14, 2025

Programme

  • 10:30–11:00  Welcome coffee
  • 11:00–11:30  Official begin and short introduction round: experiences, needs, and open issues for planned research
  • 11:30–12:00  Input: Greta Jasser, Political Science and social network research, Univ. Göttingen
  • 12:00–12:30  Input: Hella von Unger, Research ethics in social sciences and social media, LMU München
  • 12:30–13:00  Lunch
  • 13:00–13:30  Input: Andreas Wiebe (online), Law, data protection, and social media research, Univ. Göttingen
  • 13:30–15:00  Breakout sessions
  • 15:00–16:00  Summary of breakout sessions, to-dos, and future plans
